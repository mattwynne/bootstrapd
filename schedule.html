Bootstrapd will be an open-space event, which means we all get to decide the programme. We'd like to see some of the brainstorming and decision-making about topics and sessions happen beforehand, via the mailing list.

The conference starts at 9:30am with lightning talks from everyone who wants to lead a session, then we'll build a first draft of the programme as post-it notes on a whiteboard. You'll be encouraged to continue to modify the programme throughout the day.

Open space is all about the participants - we need you to get involved!
