The Schedule

Bootstrapd will be an open-space event on the day, but we'd like to see most of the brainstorming and decision-making about topics and sessions happen beforehand, via the mailing list.

The conference will begin at 9am with lightning talks from everyone who wants to lead a session, then we'll build a first draft of the programme as post-it notes on a whiteboard. Participants will be encouraged to continue to modify the programme throughout the day.

There will be three tracks and five session slots, making a total of 15 sessions. Throughout the day there will be one or two more rounds of lightening talks, just to stir up the mix and maybe generate new ideas for sessions during the day.

Open space is all about the participants - we need you to get involved!
